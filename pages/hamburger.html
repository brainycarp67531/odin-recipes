Hamburger

Ingredients

  • 600 g ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 pinches black pepper
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • (optional) 8 slices of cheese
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • Burger dressing

How to do it

  1. Mix the ground meat with salt, pepper, and Dijon mustard. Moisten your hands and shape 4 hamburgers (for 4 servings).
  2. Fry the hamburgers in butter and oil for 2-3 minutes on each side. If desired, add cheese when flipping the burgers.
  3. Slice the tomatoes. Peel and thinly slice the onion.
  4. Serve the hamburgers with bread, lettuce, tomatoes, onion, and preferably burger dressing and French fries (see the tip box for a recipe).
