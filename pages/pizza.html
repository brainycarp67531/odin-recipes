Pizza

Ingredients

  • 8 personal pizzas
  • 50 g yeast
  • 3 dl water
  • 8 dl wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt

Tomato Sauce

  • 500 g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Topping

  • 8 dl shredded cheese
  • Cooked shredded ham
  • Fried shredded bacon
  • Sliced salami
  • Tuna
  • Shrimp
  • Mussels
  • Onion rings
  • Mushrooms
  • Grilled bell pepper
  • Marinated artichoke hearts
  • Pepperoncini
  • Pineapple rings
  • Olives
  • Mozzarella
  • Feta cheese
  • Leftover Bolognese sauce

How to do it

  1. Crumble the yeast into a bowl. Dissolve the yeast in a small amount of water, then add the remaining water. Stir in the flour, oil, and salt.
  2. Knead the dough until smooth. Let it rise until doubled in size, about 40 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 250Â°C.
  4. Divide the dough into eight equal parts (for 8 pizzas). Knead each portion and let them rise for another 10 minutes.
  5. Roll out each piece into a round pizza base on baking paper, about 20 cm in diameter and 2 mm thick.
  6. Tomato Sauce: Bring the crushed tomatoes to a boil with the remaining tomato sauce ingredients. Leftover tomato sauce can be used in soups, stews, or Bolognese sauce, so save it!
  7. Spread the tomato sauce and sprinkle cheese on top. About 2 1/2 tbsp of tomato sauce and 1 dl of grated cheese per pizza is ideal.
  8. Let everyone choose their own toppings (about 1 1/2 dl topping per pizza is ideal). Drizzle 1 tsp of olive oil over the toppings.
  9. Heat a baking sheet in the hot oven and transfer the first pizza onto it using the baking paper.
  10. Bake the pizzas at the top of the oven for a few minutes. Remove the baking paper with the finished pizza and quickly transfer the next pizza onto the baking sheet. Repeat with all the pizzas.
